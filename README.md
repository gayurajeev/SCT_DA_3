
IBM HR Analytics — Employee Attrition Dashboard
Problem Statement

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations, leading to increased recruitment costs, loss of productivity, and reduced operational efficiency.

The objective of this project is to analyze IBM HR employee data to understand:

Why employees are leaving the company
Which departments and roles have the highest attrition
What factors influence employee turnover (overtime, income, satisfaction, etc.)
How HR teams can identify high-risk employees and improve retention strategies
